Our wedding

A small ceremony on Friday, May 4, 2012 in Alamo Square Park, a block away from our apartment in San Francisco. The ceremony will be held toward the southeast corner of the park and will start at 2:30pm, rain or shine.

We are not obtaining permits for use of the park, a cake, folding chairs, ushers, or any of the usual trappings. It's just going to be an informal gathering, and all are welcome to attend. Please be on time. We aren't going to wait.

Afterward, we'll head over to Green Chile Kitchen, a few blocks away at McAllister & Baker for snacks, drinks, and chairs.

In lieu of the traditional reception, we will be holding a costume party later in the evening at 111 Minna. It will be a private party from 8pm-11pm, after which the venue will open to the public with dance music.
