Our wedding

A small ceremony on Friday, May 4, 2012 in Alamo Square Park, a block away from our apartment in San Francisco. The ceremony will be held toward the southeast corner of the park and will start at 2:30pm, rain or shine.

We are not obtaining a permit for use of the park, a cake, folding chairs, ushers, or any of the usual trappings. It's going to be an informal family gathering, but all are welcome to attend.

Afterward, we'll head a few blocks over to Green Chile Kitchen at McAllister & Baker for snacks, drinks, and chairs.

In lieu of the traditional reception, we will be holding an adults-only (21+) costume party later in the evening. We'll have full details soon, but the venue will be in San Francisco and the party will start around 8pm.

Please, no gifts. Seriously! We already have more Crate & Barrel porcelain popcorn buckets for a fun movie night at home than we know what to do with.

We know Friday afternoon isn't the most convenient time. Please come to the park if you can make it, but the celebration is the most important thing to us. We hope all of you can make it to the party. In costume.
